A Letter to Dad

Dear Dad,

I know we don't always say it out loud — but I want you to know, today and every day, how much you mean to me.

Those 10 days when it was just us? I think about that a lot. You made sure I was okay. You were fun and present and just… Dad. That's something I'll carry with me for the rest of my life.

And the day you walked into that school and stood up for me — that wasn't just a parent moment. That was a superhero moment. You didn't have to make a big deal out of it, but you did, because to you, I was worth it.

You've always told me: "Working hard is always better than being smart." I've thought about that so many times. It's not just advice — it's the way you live your life. And I see it. I respect it. I want to be like that.

And of course — thank you for the flowers. 😂 You've made life lighter and funnier than it had any right to be.

You are warm, you are strong, you are funny, and you are mine. I'm so lucky you're my dad.

With all my love,
Om 💛
